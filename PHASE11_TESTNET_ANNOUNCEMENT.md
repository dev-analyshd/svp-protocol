2. Create or import your wallet
3. Note your wallet address for testnet funds

#### Step 2: Get Testnet Funds
Choose your preferred network and get testnet funds:

**Arbitrum Sepolia**:
- Visit: https://faucet.arbitrum.io/
- Enter your wallet address
- Claim 0.5 ETH
- Wait for confirmation (usually 1-2 minutes)

**Polygon Mumbai**:
- Visit: https://faucet.polygon.technology/
- Select "Polygon Mumbai" and "ETH"
- Enter your wallet address
- Claim 0.5 ETH
- Also claim MATIC for transaction fees

- Visit: https://sepoliafaucet.com/
- Enter your wallet address
- Claim 0.5+ ETH
- Wait for confirmation

#### Step 3: Access the dApp
1. Visit: https://testnet.svpprotocol.dev (once deployed)

5. Switch to desired testnet in MetaMask
6. Start testing!
